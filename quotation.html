The heart of the project.

Item
Homepage search bar. A beautiful "What's been troubling you?" entry point, placed via a simple shortcode so it sits exactly where you want.
Calm chat experience (custom app). The visitor is taken into a soft, brand-matched conversation screen — fully designed, not a generic chatbot box.
The AI brain — Paramjit's voice & method. Trained on the context, therapies and packages you provide, so it responds the way Paramjit would: warm, soft, never clinical or pushy.
Voice input — speak instead of type. Visitors can simply talk to the AI; their speech is understood and answered, so people in distress who don't feel like typing can still be met gently.
Value first, then gentle invitation. The AI first understands the person and offers genuine help, then softly offers the free 30-minute discovery call and suggests the most relevant programme.
Issue → programme matching. The AI maps what the person shares to the right offering (migraine, anxiety, relationships, yoga therapy, fertility) and the right next step.
OpenAI integration, done safely. Connected to OpenAI on a secure server — your API key is never exposed in the browser.
Care & safety guardrails. The AI never diagnoses or gives medical advice, and if someone is in real distress it gently pauses and points them to professional help-lines.
Admin dashboard — your control room. A private dashboard for you and Paramjit to monitor every conversation, see captured leads, and add, edit or re-price the healing packages the AI offers — no developer needed.
